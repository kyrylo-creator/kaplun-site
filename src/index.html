Approach

How I work

01

PPT Method

Positive and Transcultural Psychotherapy: we rely not only on the problem but also on your strengths and resources. The approach takes the cultural context into account — particularly valuable when adapting abroad.

02

Mind & body together

As a yoga and Taoist-practice teacher, I combine talking work with attention to the body and breath. Lasting balance is born at the intersection of these two dimensions.

03

A safe space

No judgement — only warmth, respect and confidentiality. You set the pace; my job is to be there with you and help you find your way.
